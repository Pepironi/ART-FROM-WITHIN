Aenon Rose Patron

Aenon Rose, a 20-year-old student and freelance photographer, developed a passion for photography in middle school. Receiving her first DSLR camera at age 14, she began to pursue photography professionally. She enjoys capturing memories in her divers and creative style, particularly street and sports photography. In November 2023, she exhbited her works for the first time at the LPU-Manila's "Silakbo: An Outburst of Artistic Desire" exhibition.
