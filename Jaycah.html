Jaycah Roncales

Jaycah Roncales is a 21-year-old Multimedia Arts student. She started making art traditionally using watercolor, gouache, and acrylic paint. Now she is exploring digital arts and has started making a few digital illustrations. She also enjoys doing 3D modeling and 3D animation. She likes watching YouTube videos while doing something productive, and she sees watching dramas or series as a reward for finishing a task.
