Learny Mae Tabozo

Learny's childhood dream was to become an illustrator. When she was a senior in high school, she started improving herself at traditional art before learning about digital art. After receiving a pen and tablet from her older sister, she gradually developed an interest in splash arts. She worked on her art every single day as a burning desire, and eventually she made the decision to enter numerous contests to draw tablet firms. She emerged victorious in a competition. Last year, she worked at Studio Sekai as an artist. She created one hundred illustrations to encourage their community to participate and eagerly await more incredible news about their upcoming series. Due to financial difficulties at home, she dropped out of college and found a job in her specialty that she enjoys. She got hired as an artist at the studio despite her young age. She was the youngest, and her incredible creations were inspired by her intense love of creating art. She then took charge of the community art after meeting several talented artists who assisted her in improving her illustrations. She worked at Studio Sekai as one of the pioneers . As the main community artist, Learny has been essential in giving the Isekai universe's numerous characters new lease on life. Right now, she was decided to end her contract working in sekai and currently having a small business of making bracelets and keychains. She was also decided to go back at college to finish her degree while working to other studio as colorist webtoon.
