Jeramie Padilla

During my childhood, limited resources sparked my creativity. Rather than asking for toys, I created my own, igniting my lifelong passion for art. My earliest projects involved transforming everyday items, such as clothespins turning it into imaginative creations like rocket ships, doing cut-out paperdolls, oregami and spent countless hours on art and craft projects inspired by my favorite art shows and cartoons. As time passed, I found myself increasingly drawn to art. I consider myself a "Jack of all trades," having explored and experimented with various mediums and techniques, mastering none. My journey is a testament to the idea that true art comes from within. The process of creating something from nothing, driven by pure imagination and resourcefulness.
